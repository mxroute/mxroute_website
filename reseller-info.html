What is Reseller Service?

Understanding how MXroute's reseller service differs from our standard email hosting plans.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notice

Reseller is not as polished as our normal service, because we give you tutorials to turn it into your own service in the way that we've done with ours. It's a blank template, and if you want to just jump right in without setting it up for your business, you might be disappointed.

Service Comparison

Standard MXroute Service

When you purchase normal MXroute service, you receive:

  • One control panel login to a server
  • Ability to add domains and email accounts
  • Limited capabilities such as:
    • Cannot give customers direct control panel logins
    • Cannot automate reselling with billing software
    • Cannot hide MXroute's URL/logo

Reseller Service

Reseller service provides:

  • Same core MXroute service
  • Higher-level permissions
  • Ability to:
    • Create separate control panel accounts for each customer
    • Give customers unique logins to manage their own domains/email
    • Customize control panel appearance (style, layout, logo)
    • Hide login behind your own domain (e.g., mailadmin.yourdomain.com)

Key Characteristics

  • Requires manual configuration of customer packages
  • Not ready-to-use - needs setup work before customers can use it
  • Provides a "blank template" that you must customize for your business
  • Includes storage limit settings and user management tools
  • Comes with tutorials to guide you through the setup process
  • Less polished than standard service - designed for customization

‚ö†Ô∏è Setup Required

Unlike our standard email hosting that works immediately, reseller service requires you to:

  • Follow setup tutorials to configure the service
  • Customize the control panel for your brand
  • Set up customer packages and billing integration
  • Configure your own domain for customer logins

If you're looking for plug-and-play email hosting, choose our standard service instead.

Recommended For

Reseller service is ideal for users who want to:

  • Delegate control of service portions to customers
  • Create a white-labeled email hosting platform
  • Offer email hosting services under their own brand
  • Provide customers with direct access to manage their own email accounts
